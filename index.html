01 — Brief & Objective

The strategic foundation

A clear Get–To–By framework anchors every concept to a single business outcome.

GET
Building professionals (builders, architects, designers) and homeowners who value trust, credibility, and efficiency. Professionals want quality leads that convert. Homeowners want verified experts they can confidently hire. Both are frustrated by unreliable directories and unvetted platforms.
TO
Make BuildStart their go-to platform for discovering, connecting, and managing building projects — the trusted space where credible professionals and serious clients meet to get work done.
BY
Positioning BuildStart as the credibility-first construction community — a smarter, simpler, and safer way to find and collaborate with verified professionals. Through AI-assisted project tools, verified profiles, chat, and review systems, users experience a seamless journey from idea to completion.
The audience
  • Architects, builders & designers seeking quality leads
  • Homeowners seeking verified, reliable experts
  • Trust-led decision makers who rely on peer validation
The problem we solve

Unreliable directories create friction and risk on both sides. BuildStart replaces uncertainty with a credibility-first, AI-powered network where every professional is verified before they appear.

Campaign objectives
  • Drive professional sign-ups & verified profiles
  • Build brand trust with homeowner audience
  • Position BuildStart as the category leader in AU

Concept 1 — The Orbit

A single glowing screen floats in darkness — the camera orbits it cinematically, revealing a new professional each time it passes behind the monitor, making BuildStart the gravitational centre of the entire industry.

Treatment summary

Premium 360° orbit camera technique in a dark cyclorama — one platform, multiple lives revolving around it. Cinematic, filmic, high-contrast.

Core idea / creative magnifier

The orbit camera makes BuildStart feel like the gravitational centre connecting all professionals — everyone revolves around the same platform.

The dark-vs-light contrast positions BuildStart as premium and cutting-edge, not a job board.

Concept 3 — Split Screen

Two architects, two mindsets — Anna thrives on BuildStart while Ben watches his phone stay silent, until he finally joins.

Treatment summary

Split-screen contrast — Anna vs Ben, BuildStart vs doing nothing — viewers instantly see themselves in either character and feel the cost of not being on the platform.

Core idea / creative magnifier

The format creates aspiration (be Anna) and FOMO (don't be Ben) simultaneously. Ben's punchline "Should've BuildStarted sooner" is a rare self-aware brand moment that lands as humour while sealing the value proposition.

03 — Production Requirements

Lean, efficient, premium

All three concepts are engineered for a single-day shoot with one actor and one location — maximising quality while controlling cost.

🎬
Location
  • Single dark cyclorama studio
  • One hero setup: desk + glowing screen
  • Lighting creates separation between character worlds
  • Premium look without multiple locations
🎭
Talent
  • 1 actor, multiple roles via camera movement
  • No additional cast required
  • Performance-driven, minimal dialogue
  • VO narrator recorded separately
⚙️
Logistics
  • Key focus: controlled 360° orbit rig / dolly
  • Precise blocking + choreography required
  • Heavier emphasis on camera + lighting vs set
  • Post includes UI overlays + seamless transitions
🎬
Location
  • Single bright studio, modular styling
  • Small prop swaps + lighting shifts suggest environments
  • No multiple locations needed — cost-efficient
🎭
Talent
  • 1 actor playing multiple roles
  • Differentiated through wardrobe + simple props
  • VO narrator recorded separately
⚙️
Logistics
  • Transition-led filming: in-camera + edit-driven
  • Light UI overlays: dashboard + notifications
  • Minimal crew, fast 1-day shoot
  • Moderate post for transitions + graphics
🎬
Location
  • Single studio split into two contrasting setups
  • Clean/bright (Anna) vs cluttered/dim (Ben)
  • Built side-by-side or achieved through framing
🎭
Talent
  • 1 actor playing both roles
  • Simple wardrobe + styling changes
  • Performance contrast carries the story
⚙️
Logistics
  • Locked-off camera for split-screen consistency
  • Minimal movement — easier shoot
  • Heavier reliance on post: split screen + UI overlays
  • Optional banter end tag as pickup
